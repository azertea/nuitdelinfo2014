What is Hope ?

Hope is a website created to allow people which has been separated due to disasters or wars to look for their familly or friends.

How can I use Hope ?

Once you've created a profile on our website (and completed your physical description), another user can found you by using our powerful search engine. In order to protect your confidentiality, this user will only receive a message which confirm that a profile has been found. After that you will receive an email with the full profile description of the person who want to find you, and then you're free to get in touch with him.

NGO agents has specific rights that allow them to create profile for people hosted in their camp. If your search matches a NGO-created profile, we will send an email to this agent. They can also make a search an access directly matched profiles.
